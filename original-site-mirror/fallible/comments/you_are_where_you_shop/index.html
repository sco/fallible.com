You Are Where You Shop

"I love your skirt," said the lady in line next to me at Starbucks. "Where did you get it?"

You've got to know that "my" Starbucks is mere minutes away from the ritziest shopping district in Johnson County, Kansas.

"Walmart," I answered, with a friendly smile.

"Oh....well....I tried on one just like it at Talbot's, but it didn't fit right...."

"Mine fits right," I said.

"Ummm...I kind of like Target," she said, backing away.

Was it something I said?

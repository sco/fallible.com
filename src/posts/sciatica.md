---
title: "Sciatica"
date: "2012-07-10T22:52:00"
---

O, horrible capacious cries,  
She damns her waves of pain.  
And then she counts this travesty  
Above her greatest bane.  
Sciatica! Sciatica!  
O, why did you choose me?  
Screamed when I stood,  
Not in the mood,  
Pinched nerve from butt to knee!

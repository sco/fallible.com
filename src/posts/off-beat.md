Two clocks like electric shocks  
Only not set to Greenwich time  
One ticks as the other tocks,  
Committing a second-by-second crime.

Two plans for a given day  
Compete like a pair of off-beat clocks  
One plan to go and one to stay,  
But both to wear slippers without socks.
